# Negative Findings

Important negative results include:

- repeated Phillips–Ouliaris failures in earlier FM-OLS specifications;
- large and reset-sensitive coefficient magnitudes;
- integration-order risk in generated interaction variables;
- instability across windows;
- mismatch between appealing coefficient signs and admissible residual behavior;
- weak support for treating short event windows as independent regimes.

These failures motivated redesign and must not be forgotten.
