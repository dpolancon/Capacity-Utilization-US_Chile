|---|---|---|
| accumulated means of production | ME + NRC productive stock | GPIM construction |
| productive capacity | integrated reconstructed path | latent and anchored |

| distributional state | wage share or related variable | boundary-sensitive |
| technique/composition | interaction or accumulated interaction | generated regressor |
| capacity-building elasticity | \(\theta_t\) | coefficient-derived |
| external constraint | reserves, leverage, current account, financing | Chile-specific |
| profitability | utilization × capacity productivity × profit share | boundary coherence |
