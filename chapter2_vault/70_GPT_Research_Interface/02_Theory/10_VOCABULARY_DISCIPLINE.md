# Vocabulary Discipline

- **Productive capacity:** latent output potential of the installed productive structure.
- **Potential output:** acceptable only when explicitly equated to reconstructed productive capacity.
- **Utilization:** actual relative to reconstructed capacity.
- **Transformation relation:** long-run mapping from accumulation and state variables to capacity.
- **Coherence:** accounting, boundary, stock-flow, and theoretical compatibility.
- **Consistency:** statistical convergence or replication.
- **Anchor:** normalization fixing a level constant.
- **Diagnostic:** reveals imbalance.
- **Classifier:** activates a regime.
