# The I(2) Trap

If \(x_t\) and \(z_t\) are I(1), their product or accumulated interaction may be I(2).

Estimating:

\[
y_t=\beta_1x_t+\beta_2z_t+\beta_3x_tz_t+u_t
\]

as an ordinary I(1) cointegrating regression can invalidate the assumed asymptotics.

The project therefore treats the interaction structure as a polynomial-cointegration problem rather than a cosmetic nonlinear term.
