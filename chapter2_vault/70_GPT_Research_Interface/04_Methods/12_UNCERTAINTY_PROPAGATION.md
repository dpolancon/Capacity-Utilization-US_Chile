# Uncertainty Propagation

Uncertainty travels through:

\[
\widehat\beta
\rightarrow \widehat\theta_t
\rightarrow \Delta\widehat y_t^p
\rightarrow \widehat y_t^p
\rightarrow \widehat\mu_t
\rightarrow \widehat r_t.
\]

A full implementation may use simulation, bootstrap, delta-method approximations, or fixed-\(b\) procedures. The chapter must at minimum state which uncertainty is and is not propagated.
