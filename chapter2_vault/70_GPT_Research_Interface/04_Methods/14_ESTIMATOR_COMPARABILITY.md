# Estimator Comparability

FM-OLS, DOLS, and IM-OLS are comparable when they estimate the same admissible long-run relation.

They are not automatically comparable with a CPR specification if the latter changes:

- integration order;
- coefficient combination;
- normalization;
- polynomial cointegrating vector;
- sample or deterministic structure.

The methods section must state what remains invariant across estimators.
