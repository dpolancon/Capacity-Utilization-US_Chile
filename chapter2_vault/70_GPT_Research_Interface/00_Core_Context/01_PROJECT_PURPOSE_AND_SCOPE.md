# Project Purpose and Scope

## Purpose

Estimate a historically changing transformation relation between capital accumulation and productive capacity, reconstruct utilization, and compare center and periphery.

## Included

- U.S. and Chile annual macroeconomic reconstruction.
- Capital-stock measurement.
- Distribution and technique interaction.
- Cointegration and long-run estimation.
- Profitability decomposition.
- External-constraint architecture for Chile.
- Dissertation prose and reproducibility.

## Excluded

- Direct estimation of physical engineering capacity.
- Treating filtered output as the main capacity measure.
- Generic panel estimation across countries.
- Automatic reuse of official utilization surveys as the latent capacity object.
