- Productive capacity defined as latent.
- Utilization derived from actual relative to reconstructed capacity.
- Time-varying capital-to-capacity elasticity introduced.
- Distribution linked to technique/composition rather than added as a free-standing control.

## Phase 2 — U.S. provider and source construction

- BEA/FRED menu built.

## Phase 3 — GPIM construction and repair

## Phase 4 — Econometric exploration

- Integration-order risks identified.
- FM-OLS, DOLS, and IM-OLS compared.
- Residual cointegration failures documented.
- Johansen/VECM moved out of the primary architecture.

## Phase 5 — Interactive/polynomial cointegration

- I(2) interaction problem recognized.
- CPR/polynomial cointegration elevated in the methods architecture.
- Interaction timing and accumulated-index logic clarified.

## Phase 6 — Reconstruction

- Specification A and B compared.

- Growth-law integration adopted.
- U.S. utilization reconstructed under a 1973 level anchor.

## Phase 7 — Repository and writing reorganization
