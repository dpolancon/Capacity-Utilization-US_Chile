# Major Transitions

## From observed capacity proxies to latent reconstruction

The project moved away from survey or trend proxies as the defining object.

## From fixed elasticity to changing transformation relation

The capacity effect of accumulation became state-dependent.

## From broad fixed assets to ME + NRC

The productive-capacity stock was narrowed to a theoretically coherent asset boundary.

## From VECM-first to long-run single-relation identification

Johansen/VECM became legacy or robustness material.

## From ordinary cointegration to CPR awareness

The project recognized that integrated interactions may be higher-order objects.

## From fitted level to integrated capacity-growth path

The capacity path is reconstructed through the growth law and then anchored.
