# Profitability Objects

A profit rate requires:

- numerator definition;
- capital denominator;
- sector boundary;
- gross or net treatment;
- price basis;
- tax and interest treatment;
- proprietor-income treatment.

The intended S50 corridor uses:

\[
r=\mu b\pi,
\]

but the exact empirical definitions of \(b\) and \(\pi\) must be locked in the stage specification.
