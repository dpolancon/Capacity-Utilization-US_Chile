# Boundary Registry

Every object must specify:

- domestic or national basis;
- institutional sector;
- corporate or NFC scope;
- private or total economy;
- asset inclusion;
- gross, productive, or wealth concept;
- numerator and denominator boundaries.

Baseline U.S. capital is NFC-oriented productive capital. Robustness exercises using corporate or total-economy output must be labeled as mixed-boundary designs.
