# Capital Objects

## Baseline

\[
K_{\text{capacity}}=ME+NRC.
\]

## Excluded from baseline

- IPP.
- Residential capital.
- Government transportation assets.
- Total fixed assets without decomposition.
- Land and financial assets.

## Distinctions

- Surviving gross stock.
- Productive stock.
- Wealth/net stock.
- Book value.

The project’s productive-capacity interpretation requires explicit age-efficiency logic.
