# Cointegration Source Map

- Phillips & Hansen: FM-OLS.
- Phillips & Loretan: long-run equilibrium estimation.
- Stock & Watson: DOLS and higher-order systems.
- Saikkonen: efficient cointegrating regression.
- Phillips 1995: general fully modified systems.
- Vogelsang & Wagner: IM-OLS and fixed-\(b\) inference.

The CPR architecture may require additional dedicated polynomial-cointegration sources in the live repo.
