# Source Reliability Tiers

## Tier 1

Primary official documentation and original econometric papers.

## Tier 2

Foundational theoretical and measurement sources.

## Tier 3

Reviewed internal methods notes and stage specifications.

## Tier 4

Exploratory reports, presentations, and AI-assisted research.

## Tier 5

Chat summaries and memory.

Lower tiers route research; they do not override higher tiers.
