# Networked Bundle Hub

## Purpose
This bundle joins the **US Militarized Fordism** pack and the **Copper Dynamic Relational** pack into a single Obsidian-ready network.

Its job is not to collapse the two packs into one undifferentiated archive.
Its job is to preserve their distinct internal logics while making the cross-pack articulation explicit and navigable.

## Bundle logic
Read the bundle as a two-pack machine:

US militarized demand / strategic-state priorities
→ [[USMF_05_Relation_to_Copper_Cluster]]
→ [[COP_06_Relation_to_USMF_Pack]]
→ refining / trade / stockpiling / appropriation conflict
→ Chile–US relational political economy

And in the reverse direction:

copper as material corridor
→ constraints and channels of strategic-material dependence
→ concrete sectoral mediation of U.S. Cold War capitalism
→ specification of the U.S. pole beyond generic Fordism

## Guardrail
This bundle is **networked**, not merged into a single master narrative.
The U.S. pack should not swallow the copper pack.
The copper pack should not reduce the U.S. side to mere background.
Cross-pack movement should flow through the official bridge notes and routing rules.
