# Rivas — Qualified Inclusion in Mining Package

## Inclusion rule
Rivas is included in the mining package as a **qualified secondary asset**, not as a governing theoretical framework.

## What is admissible
Rivas is admissible for:
- Part II empirical architecture on mining rent, profit-rate estimation, stock construction, investment assembly, and appropriation channels.
- Annex and table material that helps reconstruct long-run mining series, comparative stock estimates, and descriptive periodization.
- Historiographic positioning as a high-commitment rent-centered interpretation of Chile.

## What is not adopted
The dissertation's philosophical superstructure — especially its language of alienated social bonds, totalized political forms, and world-capital ontology — is **not** imported into this project's analytical shell.

## How to cite/use it
When used in chapter writing or package notes, Rivas should be introduced with a qualifier such as:

> Rivas is used selectively for rent/profit-rate measurement architecture and compiled mining series, while his broader philosophical framework is not adopted here.

## Required limitations note
Any use of Rivas should flag at least four limitations:
1. Stock results depend on valuation and depreciation choices.
2. Depreciation assumptions are partly synthetic and partly judgment-based.
3. Some mining investment and capital-stock series are acknowledged by Rivas himself as approximations or first exercises.
4. The move from measurement to political-totality interpretation is not self-validating and requires independent mechanism.

## Mandatory translation protocol
Before any numerical series, method, or claim from Rivas enters the dissertation, it must be translated into the explicit language of:
- stock concept: gross/net;
- valuation: historical cost / constant-dollar / replacement cost;
- law of motion: PIM or GPIM recursion;
- depreciation/depletion convention;
- sector boundary;
- source and benchmark year.

## Practical use in the mining package
Inside the package, Rivas should sit in three places only:
- as a **critical-use reading note**;
- as a **measurement quarry** for long-run mining series;
- as a **contrast case** against more analytically disciplined notes such as [[ShaikhBasu__GPIM_Method_Note]].

## Working doctrine
Extract the empirical apparatus. Quarantine the ontology.
