# Copper Relational Cluster — Dynamic Hub

## Purpose
This is the dynamic counterpart to the static copper relational cluster.
Its job is to animate the copper field as a moving relation rather than a sector archive.

## Governing principle
Copper is treated here as a **material corridor** linking:
- U.S. militarized demand and strategic-state priorities
- refining, processing, trade, and stockpiling circuits
- multinational control and appropriation struggles
- Chilean state formation, accumulation, and conflict
