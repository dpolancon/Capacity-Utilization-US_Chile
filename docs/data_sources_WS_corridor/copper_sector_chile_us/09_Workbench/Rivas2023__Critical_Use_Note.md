## One-sentence function
This is a **data-rich but theoretically overcommitted dissertation** to be mined selectively.

## Core claim
Rivas seeks to explain Chilean specificity through mining land rent, world capitalism, and the political forms of class struggle, combining quantitative estimation with a strong systematic-dialectical vocabulary.

## Why it matters for this project
It likely contains useful long-run empirical architecture on profit rates, mining stocks, investment, depreciation conventions, and rent-flow estimates.

## Strongest extractable mechanisms
- long-run mining and total profit-rate construction;
- mining investment and stock-series assembly;
- rent-flow periodization;
- historical decomposition of appropriation channels.

## Best use in writing
Use as a **measurement quarry** and a **historiographic foil**.

## Limits / warnings
Do not let its ontology govern the project. Its philosophical architecture is not the required shell for using its empirical material.
