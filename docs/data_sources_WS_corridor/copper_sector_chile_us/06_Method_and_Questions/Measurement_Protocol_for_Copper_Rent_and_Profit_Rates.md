# Measurement Protocol for Copper Rent and Profit Rates

## Governing doctrine
Before any rent or profit-rate claim is accepted into the drafting layer, state explicitly:
- numerator,
- denominator,
- stock concept,
- valuation basis,
- depreciation/depletion assumption,
- sector boundary,
- time window,
- source chain.

## Required checklist

### 1. Flow side
- output or value added?
- gross or net surplus?
- before or after taxes?
- business only or broader institutional coverage?

### 2. Stock side
- gross or net capital stock?
- historical cost, constant-dollar, or replacement cost?
- aggregate or asset-specific revaluation?

### 3. Boundary side
- mining only, copper only, total mining, or total economy?
- state-owned and private capital treated symmetrically?
- non-business sectors excluded?

### 4. Interpretation side
- descriptive magnitude?
- comparative indicator?
- hypothesis test input?
- historiographic support only?

## Use rule for Rivas
Rivas enters the argument only after this checklist has been completed.

## Use rule for Shaikh/Basu
Shaikh/Basu is the default formal restatement language for stock construction in this pack.
