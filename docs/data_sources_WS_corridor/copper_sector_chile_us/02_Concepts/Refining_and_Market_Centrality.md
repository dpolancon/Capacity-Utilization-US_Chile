# Refining and Market Centrality

The copper relation is not exhausted by mine ownership. Refining, smelting, shipping, and market intermediation matter just as much.

## Working proposition
US power in copper developed not only through domestic output, but through its position as:
- processor of foreign ores and blister copper,
- refiner and re-export hub,
- market-setting node,
- organizer of international producer coordination.
