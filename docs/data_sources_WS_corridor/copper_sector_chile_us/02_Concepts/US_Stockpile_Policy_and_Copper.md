# US Stockpile Policy and Copper

US stockpile policy belongs in the corpus because it shows that copper markets were shaped not only by firms and prices, but also by the strategic apparatus of the national-security state.

## Key point
A domestic US stockpile program could alter:
- world demand conditions,
- effective price behavior,
- procurement patterns,
- incentives for producers and refiners,
- the wider international copper industry.
