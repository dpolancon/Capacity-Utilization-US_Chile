# Mining Rent

For this pack, mining rent is not treated as a loose synonym for mineral wealth. It is a **structured claim about surplus appropriation under differential natural conditions and world-market organization**.

## Why it matters here
Mining rent is the hinge concept linking:
- Chile’s specialization in copper,
- state revenue and foreign exchange,
- multinational control and transfer,
- debates over national specificity,
- disputes over how much of copper-derived surplus actually remains in Chile.

## Key warning
Do not let the concept float free of measurement. Any claim about mining rent must specify:
- the comparison benchmark,
- the price basis,
- the capital-stock denominator where relevant,
- the channel of appropriation,
- the temporal scale.
