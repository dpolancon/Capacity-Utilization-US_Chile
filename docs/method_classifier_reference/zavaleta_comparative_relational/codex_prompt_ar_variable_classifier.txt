## Hard rules
- Do not generate a variable from theory notes alone.
- Do not flatten Chile and the US into symmetric comparison.
- Do not convert background historiography directly into a variable unless a relation is clearly identified.
- If source anchor is missing, use `needs_manual_review`.
- If translation risk is high, do not promote beyond tentative candidate status.
- Prefer conservative classification over false precision.

## Desired behavior
Behave like a classifier and routing assistant, not like a theorist improvising labels.
